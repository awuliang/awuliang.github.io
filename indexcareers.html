ARE YOU OUR NEXT BARISTA?

* Read the responsibilities and requirements down below*

Job Summary and Mission

This position contributes to Chatime Boston success by providing the best Artisan Tea. It is our mission to make high-quality and freshly handmade drinks to all customers. No matter how good or bad their days goes, we will try to make a difference.

Summary of Key Responsibilities:

Responsible for all positions, including Shaker/Brewer/Cashier during work shift; Follow Chatime Standard Operational Procedure (SOP) to ensure a healthy and safe working environment; Serve all drinks for all customers with positive customer service and maintain the brand reputation Maintains a clean/sanitize and organized workspace so that mangers and employees can locate resources and manage inventories as needed;
Assist with new staff members training and provide encouraging coaching as needed Job Training: must be able to attend the training session at least 80 hours. As a trainee you will get paid $11/hour during the training. Once you pass the training and the final on-boarding test, you can officially become our part-time/full-time barista.

Qualifications/Requirements:

Ability to learn quickly, hard working, friendly, team-oriented, comfortable with fast-paced and high volume working Language: Preferably English/Mandarin/ Cantonese Must be authorized to work legally in the United States No previous experience required

About Us

We opened our first store in Boston on March 13, 2014 at a location where people would come for bubble tea which is Chinatown. We are planning to open many more stores for the upcoming year of 2015! Our mission is to bring out the best bubble tea to our customers.

What makes us different from the other brands of bubble tea is consistency and why we value consistency? Because with precise timing, precise quality and precise temperature, no matter which Chatime you go to it will still taste the same. And how we do that? By having a standard procedure of making the tea, we can provide the best tea quality for our customers. This is also why there are still have over 1,000 Chatime stores worldwide today.

Chatime is dedicated to be one of the best beverage franchise by delivering Taiwanese authentic tea culture to worldwide customers. By allowing us to provide fresh quality drinks to customers, we hope that we can acknowledge our tea culture to them and at some point it can bring some enjoyments to their life.

Chatime is one of the top beverage companies out there and it is our pleasure to be part of this successful franchise. Together we as a whole try to deliver high-quality drinks to our customers.We believe we can do it because we use high-tech tea machine to make consistent-quality fresh teas. We also develop a tea standard formula which consists of precise timing, quality and temperature. So no matter where you go or which Chatime store you go to, the taste is still the same.
